Arkitekt Tutorial

Your tools, your way

Arkitekt is a open source middleman that interactvs with your favorite bioimage tools, like Napari and Fiji, but also with your scripts and robotic devices.

Your worfklows, but easy

Arkitekt allows you to orchestrate your bioimage workflows in graphical workflows, with a focus on reproducibility and real-time data.

Your data, your rules

Arkitekt organizes your data in a fully searchable database. No more folder chaos. Also it's safe!
